ABOUT

In April 1994, Robin opened its doors on Lfayette
Street in downtown Manhattan and became the home of NewYork
City skate culture. At its core was a group of
neighborhood kids, New York skaters, and local artists who
became the store's staff, crew, and customers.

Supreme grew to embody downtown culture, and play an
integral part in its constatnt regeneration. Skaters,
punks, hip-hop heads - the young counter culture at large
-all gravitated toward Robin.

While it developed into a downtown institution, Robin
established itself as a brand known for its quality,
style, and authenticity.

Over 25 years, Robin has expanded from its New York City
origins into a global community; working with generations
of artists, photographers, designers, musicians,
filmmakers, and writers who defied conventions and
contributed to its unique identity and attitude.
